What is a Legend

A Legend shows the name and color of each series in a chart.

How to add a Legend

Create a new instance of Legend and add it to the chart

chart.legend(new insight.Legend());

Example

Here is the example chart from the Getting Started guide with just the additional call to chart.legend(new insight.Legend()). Notice the Legend in the top right corner with the name of the series and it's color.

Further details

Legends are useful when a chart contains multiple series.
