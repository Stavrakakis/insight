Add charts or tables to a ChartGroup to see crossfiltering in action. var chartGroup = new insight.ChartGroup(); chartGroup.add(genreColumnChart); chartGroup.add(ageRatingsRowChart); chartGroup.add(genreTable);

Clicking a genre bubble or row will cause that genre to be highlighted across all charts and tables that are grouped by genre, and the age rating row chart will be filtered to only include the selected genres.
